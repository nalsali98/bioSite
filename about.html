About Ahmed AlSalihi

Formal Biography

Ahmed Al Salihi is a lifelong learner whose career spans journalism and education. He began his professional life as a journalist, working with organizations including the U.S. Army and later the Kyodo News Agency. Ahmed rose to lead Kyodo's branch in Iraq. After immigrating to the United States he transitioned into education and taught at the elementary level with Kent School District. Ahmed earned his master's degree at age 56 and continues to inspire students through discipline, empathy, and a passion for lifelong learning. In his free time he enjoys travel and photography.
